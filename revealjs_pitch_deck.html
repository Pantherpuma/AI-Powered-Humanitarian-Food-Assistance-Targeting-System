Value Proposition

We fuse satellite, climate, conflict, market, and community data to produce real-time vulnerability maps that enable smarter, faster humanitarian food assistance.

The Problem

  • Growing food insecurity in conflict & climate‑impacted regions.
  • Manual assessments are slow and outdated.
  • Data sources are fragmented.
  • Communities lack channels to report urgent needs.

Why Existing Systems Fall Short

  • Outdated or incomplete datasets.
  • No integrated real-time analysis.
  • Weak community reporting tools.
  • Lack of unified vulnerability mapping.

Our Solution

  • AI Vulnerability Index (multi-source data fusion)
  • Offline-first mobile reporting app
  • Interactive web dashboard
  • Predictive analytics & early warnings
  • Logistics & delivery planning

Technology & Architecture

  • Backend: FastAPI, Python, PostGIS, ML models
  • Mobile: React Native (offline support)
  • Web: React, Mapbox maps
  • Integrated Data: Satellite, climate, conflict, markets, community reports

Key Features

  • Real-time vulnerability heatmaps
  • AI-based risk scoring
  • Community alerts (GPS + camera)
  • Analytics for planning distributions
  • Multi-agency collaboration

Impact & Use Cases

Use cases:

  • Rapid crisis detection
  • Targeted food distribution
  • Monitoring market shocks
  • Government & NGO coordination

Impact: Faster response, reduced waste, empowered communities.

Stakeholders
